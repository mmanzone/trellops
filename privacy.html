1. Introduction

This Privacy Disclaimer outlines how Trellops (the "Service"), provided by manzone.org, handles user data in relation to the third-party service, Trello (owned by Atlassian).

Your privacy is paramount. We strive to be transparent about what data we access and, crucially, what data we do not store.

2. Data Accessed and Purpose

Trellops is a display service designed to present information and data stored within your personal Trello account.

Trello Data Access: The Service requires you to log in to your Trello account via Trello's official authentication process (using an OAuth token). This grants Trellops read-only access to the Trello boards, cards, and associated data you authorise. This access is strictly used to retrieve and display the information to you during your session.

No Data Storage on Developer Servers: Trellops does not store, copy, archive, or transmit any data retrieved from your Trello account onto our own developer servers or any cloud-based storage owned by manzone.org. All retrieved Trello information is processed in real-time solely within your web browser.

3. Local Storage and Session Management

To provide a functional and seamless user experience, Trellops utilises local storage technologies on your device (e.g., browser cookies, local storage, or session storage).

Information Stored Locally: This local storage is used to securely hold essential information necessary for the Service to operate, which may include:

  • Your Trello API/OAuth token for the duration of your session.
  • Session IDs and other temporary settings (e.g., preferred view, display preferences).

Local-Only Storage: This information is stored only on your computer or device and is not accessible by, or transmitted back to, manzone.org's servers or Trello/Atlassian beyond the initial Trello API calls necessary to fetch the data.

Security: Once you log out or close your session, this locally stored session data (including the Trello token) is typically invalidated or removed, requiring a fresh login for the next use.

4. Third-Party Links and Trello's Privacy Policy

Trellops relies entirely on the Trello platform. Any data stored in Trello is subject to Trello/Atlassian's own privacy policy and terms of service. We strongly encourage you to review their policies regarding the storage and security of your data.

5. Changes to This Disclaimer

We may update this Privacy Disclaimer from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new disclaimer on this page.
